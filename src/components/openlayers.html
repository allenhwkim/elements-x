x-openlayers

NameTypeDefaultOptionalDescription
zoomNumber15OptionalZoom level of the map
centerLngLat0,0Optional Center Lng/Lat of the map, or an adderss. e.g. Incheon International Airport
satelliteN/AN/AOptional If given, satellite layer will be visible when initialized

x-ol-marker

NameTypeDefaultOptionalDescription
positionLngLatN/ARequired Center Lng/Lat of the map, or an adderss. e.g. Incheon International Airport
centerN/AN/AOptional If given, this marker will be center of the map
