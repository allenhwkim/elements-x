x-data, Custom Formatter

Format number, currency, or date

Number

Default:
French: Default: <x-data type="number" value="1234567.8901234"></x-data> French: <x-data type="number" locale="fr-CA" max-decimal="3" value="1234567.8901234"></x-data>

Currency

Default:
French:
Korean: Default: <x-data type="currency" value="1234567.8901234"></x-data> French: <x-data type="currency" locale="fr-CA" currency="CAD" value="1234567.8901234"></x-data> Korean: <x-data type="currency" locale="ko-KR" currency="KRW" value="1234567.8901234"></x-data>

Date

Default:
French:
Custom English:
Custom French: Default: <x-data type="date" value="2021-05-29 12:34:59"></x-data> French: <x-data type="date" locale="fr-FR" value="2021-05-29 12:34:59"></x-data> Custom English: <x-data type="date" format="weekday month yyyy M d" value="2021-05-29 12:34:59"></x-data> Custom French: <x-data type="date" locale="fr-CA" format="week mon yyyy MM dd hh:mm:ss" value="2021-05-29 12:34:59">

Attributes

  • type: format type. number, currency, or date
  • data-value: value to format. e.g. 12345.67
  • locale: locale. default en-US, e.g. fr-CA (language-country)
  • max-decimal: max decimal point. Default 2. Used with type="number"
  • currency: currency. default. USD, e.g. USD, KRW, or CAD
  • format: date format. e.g, yyyy-MM-dd hh:mm:ss Default. locale date format. for en-US, mm-dd-yy
    yyyy: 4 digit year, e.g., 2021
    yy: 2 digit year, e.g., 21
    MM: 2 digit month, e.g., 05
    M: 1 or 2 digit month. e.g., 5, or 12
    dd: 2 digit day. e.g., 05, or 12
    d: 1 or digit day. e.g., 5, or 12
    hh: 2 digit hours. e.g., 05, or 23
    h: 1 or digit hours. e.g., 5, or 23
    mm: 2 digit minutes. e.g., 05, or 59
    m: 1 or digit minutes. e.g., 5, or 59
    ss: 2 digit seconds. e.g., 05, or 59
    s: 1 or digit seconds. e.g., 5, or 59
    Month: Month name. e.g. January
    Mon: Month name in short format. e.g. Jan
    Weekday: Weekday name. e.g. Wednesday
    Week: Weekday name in short format. e.g. Wed
